QAMBS

A seminar/journal club/reading group on Quantum Algorithms for Many Body Systems.

Keywords: integrability, dual-unitarity, ground state preparation, time evolution, entanglement generation and propagation, tensor networks, topological phases.

Schedule

From 11:00 to 12:00, every other Tuesday of the month, at this Zoom link. First 45 minutes: presentation of own work or a talk by an invited speaker. Remaining time: questions and discussion.
